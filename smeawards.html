2016 SME Awards

Another year of the SME Awards and we are Happy to say we have been nominated in 3 categories for the SME awards. We have been nominated in the Under 30, B2B SME Growth and I am most proud to say we have been nominated in the Community Minded category for our work with various clubs/charities.

This is a great honor because we love helping our local events and fostering some community pride, being from a rural part of Kerry, I know how much this means to a small community and I am humbled to be able to help in any way. Four of our clients have also been nominated in various categories bringing our total tally to seven!

Thanks to all the judges who nominated us! A special congratulations to all my fellow SME's, you people are awesome and finding similar people online is a daily pick me up!

The fact that Leonardo DiCaprio finally got his hands on an Oscar gives us hope that we may finally win something to place next to that U14 medal from Mosney, our nominations are firmly in the teens without a win and the mantelpiece is incredibly bare so here is hoping that our time has arrived too!

All joking aside it is a great honor to be nominated alongside so many fantastic businesses and you can check out the full list of nominees which will be whittled down to a finalists list here. You can also purchase tickets for the event which takes place in April:

http://smeawards.ie/

http://smeawards.ie/

It promises to be a great event and I am looking forward to the night to meet some great people and hopefully the Kingdom can finish the night with a winner!

We made the finalist list in the B2B SME category, thrilled to be recognised next to so many incredible businesses!

Aidan out.
