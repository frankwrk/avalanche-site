Where did the name Avalanche come from?

At Avalanche we specialise in unique web design for innovative people. The name Avalanche was chosen directly from his favourite video game when Aidan was 7, Final Fantasy VII. In the Playstation classic, Avalanche is a group that aims to change the world & we thought it was the perfect name for what we want to do! We are always looking for new projects that excite us & help us to make a real difference to people & help them reach their goals.
