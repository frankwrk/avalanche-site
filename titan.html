Titan Business Awards 2023

The Titan Business Awards, an annual awards program that recognizes outstanding businesses and individuals in various industries on an international basis, recently announced that Avalanche has won the award for Best Design Agency. This prestigious award recognizes Avalanche's commitment to excellence in web design, eCommerce solutions, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

We would like to thank every member of the grand jury who evaluated our work over the last few years, there are some amazing agencies/freelancers we have spoken with, collaborated with and even planned some future projects in the coming 12-18 months.

A big thank you to everyone on the team here as well at Avalanche, as ever, I am just a very small part of what we create every day for our clients.

Titan Judging Panel feedback:

"The Titan Business Awards are dedicated to initiate business to the forefront of credibility. They encourage all professionals from different fields to break the glass ceiling to achieve Titan like Success, where their professionalism & dedication are true testaments to their passion in business. They aspire for all professionals to go beyond the status quo, promoting their strategies, cultures & teamwork to the world.

Avalanche have proven themselves over the last decade as a business titan worldwide and have created a global platform for all businesses in the digital design space to aim for, as they continue to demonstrate their achievements in the industry.

Even then, amongst those who reign, Avalanche continually hone themselves. After more than a decade, the fact remains that they excel not just within their immediate surroundings but are sought after internationally. They are not satisfied with merely portraying excellence, but with mastering excellence itself.

They are a Titan in every aspect of the word."

Based on the legendary Mo'ai statues, the Titan Awards statuette radiates strength and sturdiness. As with a business, the multi-faceted structures depict the wide assortment of talents within the business industry that persevered through adversities and hardships.

The 13" statuette positioned on top of a solid zinc-alloy base signifies businesses with a strong foundation, built through blood, sweat, and tears.

A great addition to the mantelpiece!

Aidan out.
