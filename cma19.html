The Irish Content Marketing Awards 2019

The Irish Content Marketing Awards recognise the endeavours of the Irish marketing scene and provide a platform where the best work, by the best people for the best clients are celebrated. The gala awards ceremony and dinner is annually attended by the cream of the marketing industry, offering a unique opportunity to network and celebrate with hundreds of senior executives.

The Irish Content Marketing Awards recognise outstanding achievements in leveraging the content marketing discipline to drive business and organisational success. I am nominated for the Content Marketing Rising Star 2019 which is for those excelling beyond their tentative years.

Finalists were required to demonstrate an outstanding contribution to content marketing, learnings, skills and career progress made during the previous 12 months in order to be recognised. It is a great honour to be nominated in this prestigious category for the second year in a row and I hope we can go one further this year.

Congratulations to all of our fellow nominees and the winners will be revealed at ceremony on Thursday, September 26th, at the Ballsbridge Hotel, Dublin. As always, we look forward to throwing on the glad rags and meeting some people who are doing some fantastic work in Ireland and further afield.

You can view the full shortlist of 2019 nominees here if you are interested: https://www.irishcma.ie/shortlist

Aidan out.
