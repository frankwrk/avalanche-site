The Benefits of Improving Your Website's Domain Authority

But what is DA, and why does it matter for your website? In short, domain authority is a score that predicts how well a website will rank on search engine results pages (SERPs). Developed by Moz, DA is calculated based on a number of factors, including the quality and relevance of a website's content, the number and quality of backlinks to the site, and other technical factors like page load speed and mobile responsiveness.
